About This Project

This tool was designed to help self-employed users keep accurate digital records of their receipts for HMRC compliance.

Key Features

  • 📸 Upload receipts via camera or file picker.
  • 🔍 OCR text extraction (Tesseract.js).
  • ✅ Automated compliance checks.
  • 📊 Charts for categories and monthly totals.
  • 📤 Export to CSV and PDF.
  • 🧹 Demo data included to preview functionality.

Disclaimer: This tool is provided for guidance only. Always confirm expenses and claims with a qualified accountant before submitting to HMRC.
