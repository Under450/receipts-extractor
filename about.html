About This Project

This is a HMRC-style receipt extractor dashboard with OCR, manual entry, compliance checks, exports, and charts.

  • 📷 Upload receipts (camera or files)
  • 🧾 OCR via Tesseract.js
  • ✍ Manual entry option
  • ✅ Compliance checks
  • 📊 Charts
  • 📄 CSV/PDF exports

Disclaimer: For assistance only. Confirm with a qualified accountant for HMRC compliance.
